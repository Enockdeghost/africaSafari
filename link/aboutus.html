About True Africa Escape

With over 15 years of experience in African tourism, True Africa Escape is your trusted partner for unforgettable safari experiences. We specialize in creating personalized adventures that showcase the best of Tanzania's wildlife, culture, and natural beauty.

Our team of expert guides, drivers, and support staff are passionate about sharing their knowledge and love for Tanzania. From the vast plains of the Serengeti to the pristine beaches of Zanzibar, we'll help you create memories that will last a lifetime.
